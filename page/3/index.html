Vaibhav Lakshmi

Vaibhav Lakshmi Puja is a meaningful religious ceremony that takes place during the Vaibhava Lakshmi Vrat, each Agrahayana month. During this ritual, the Goddess of wealth, Maa Lakshmi, is invited to the house, and she is asked for her blessing. She can bestow her devotees with her protection from misfortune, happiness, beauty, and prosperity.
